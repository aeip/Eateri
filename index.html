What is Eateri?

Eateri is a comprehensive digital platform designed to revolutionize the dining experience by addressing both customer-facing and restaurant operations. Using dual apps, Eateri provides a seamless, data-driven, and personalized experience for both customers and restaurant staff. The platform leverages advanced technologies like AI and blockchain to enhance operations, improve customer satisfaction, and enable better decision-making.

Eateri Customer App

How Eateri Works

  1. Customers create profiles with preferences and allergies.
  2. Servers access customer profiles for personalized orders.
  3. Kitchen staff receive optimized order lists with urgency indicators.
  4. Inventory is automatically tracked and replenished using AI insights.

Core Functionalities

Personalized Dining and Customer Profiles

Profiles store extensive customer data, such as dietary preferences, allergy information, and order history. This information is accessible across Eateri’s network, allowing restaurants to tailor their service. By recognizing and catering to customer habits, Eateri promotes repeat visits and enhances customer loyalty.

Order Management and Fulfillment

Orders are managed in a way that improves accuracy and speed, with features like customer profile tagging, which aids servers in providing personalized recommendations and service. For cooks, Eateri displays orders with urgency indicators and distinguishes between dine-in, takeout, and delivery using color codes.

Inventory Management

AI is employed to monitor ingredient levels based on current sales data, upcoming events, and historical trends, minimizing waste and ensuring stock is available when needed. Blockchain enables supply chain transparency, recording ingredient sources and offering a verified record of quality and safety.

Blockchain and DineCoin Integration

Blockchain provides transparency, recording all ingredient sourcing details and enabling restaurants to share verifiable data with customers. DineCoin, Eateri’s cryptocurrency, is used within the platform as a loyalty reward and payment method. Customers can earn and spend DineCoins, while restaurants utilize it for accessing premium services.
